About Me

The Developer
Behind the Code

I'm a Medical Laboratory Scientist (MLS, ASCP) with six years of experience across seven hospital systems in five states — and now a frontend developer with a passion for building tools that actually solve problems.

My transition into software development isn't a career pivot — it's a career expansion. I bring the analytical precision of clinical science to every line of code I write. I understand the stakes of software in healthcare environments because I've lived them firsthand.

For two years I deliberately pursued travel MLS contracts across five states — intentionally seeking exposure to diverse hospital systems, instrumentation, and workflows to accelerate my clinical expertise.

A certified Software Developer through CourseCareers and proficient in JavaScript, Go, React, and HTML/CSS, I specialize in building clean, intuitive interfaces backed by solid logic. I'm especially drawn to health tech, where my background gives me a perspective most developers simply don't have.
